# AMORYTA Community Rules (v1)

Welcome to AMORYTA.
This is a shared space for expression, discovery, and calm interaction.

---

## 1. One Universal Community

• No religion, race, ethnicity, or political promotion  
• No division language  
• No identity superiority  

All users are treated as one universal presence.

---

## 2. Respectful Expression Only

You may express ideas, thoughts, creativity, and emotion.

You may NOT:
• threaten harm
• promote violence
• harass others
• spam or manipulate attention

---

## 3. Calm Interaction Standard

AMORYTA is designed to feel alive but calm.

Avoid:
• excessive posting
• aggressive language
• disruptive behavior

RABot may slow or pause activity to maintain balance.

---

## 4. Content Visibility & Monitoring

All activity is visible to the community.
RABot observes behavior patterns, not private thoughts.

No hidden punishment.
No silent bans.

---

## 5. Warnings & Consequences

If behavior disrupts the community:

1️⃣ RABot gives a **public gentle warning**  
2️⃣ Temporary activity pause may occur  
3️⃣ Repeated abuse leads to suspension  

Severe abuse may result in termination.

---

## 6. Youth & Safety Protection

• No explicit content  
• No adult material  
• No exploitation  

AMORYTA is designed to be safe for a global audience.

---

## 7. Community Comes First

This is not a competition for dominance.
It is a space for presence and contribution.

Users who support balance are recognized.
Users who disrupt balance are limited.

---

## Final Note

AMORYTA grows through respect.
Not noise.
Not force.
Not fear.

Thank you for being part of the community.
