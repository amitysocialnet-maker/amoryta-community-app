# AMORYTA — Public Description & Compliance Copy (v1)

---

## What AMORYTA Is

AMORYTA is a social community space designed for shared expression, creativity, and human connection.

It is not a marketplace.
It is not an investment platform.
It is not a financial service.

Users interact through ideas, activities, and community engagement in an open and transparent environment.

---

## What AMORYTA Is Not

• AMORYTA does not provide financial advice  
• AMORYTA does not promise profits or returns  
• AMORYTA does not encourage speculation  
• AMORYTA does not support gambling behavior  

Any in-app tokens are used strictly for participation, access, and community balance.

---

## Community First Design

AMORYTA is built without:
• followers  
• private messaging  
• content ranking manipulation  

Engagement is visible, shared, and guided by community activities.

---

## Safety & Moderation

All activity is monitored by RABot to maintain:
• respectful interaction  
• non-violent communication  
• age-appropriate experiences  

Content promoting harm, hate, or abuse is not allowed.

---

## Universal Values

AMORYTA does not support or promote:
• religion
• race
• ethnicity
• political ideology

All users are treated as equal participants in a shared space.

---

## Transparency

All actions in AMORYTA are visible and logged.
No hidden mechanics affect users unfairly.

Users always choose how they participate.

---

## Final Note

AMORYTA is a living community.
It grows through human presence, not pressure.

Participation is optional.
Exploration is encouraged.
